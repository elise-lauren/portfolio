The Field Museum Rebrand

Context

The Field Museum of Natural History is one of the largest natural history museums in the world. It originated from the 1889 World's Columbian Exposition in Chicago, and the museum collection now has nearly 40 million artifacts.

Mission Statement

"The Field Museum connects all of us to the natural world and the human story.”

Objective

This project examines the Field Museum's core values and explores how the brand's mark could represent the museum's many facets. The designed mark features a series of eight dynamic marks that each represent different aspects of the Field Museum's collection. Additionally, the brand aims to market the museum to a younger audience, intending to foster a love for learning in the younger generations. Overall, the project aims to market the Field Museum's wide variety of content to its audience in a welcoming and exciting way.
